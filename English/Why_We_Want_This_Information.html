Why Do We Want This Information?

In order to protect our community and prevent fraud, we have to collect a number of information such as name, last name, gender, country, phone number, e-mail address, etc.



The main reasons for collecting this information are;

  1. Preventing Cybercrime.
  2. To Prevent Possible Fraud Incidents.
  3. Providing People with a Better and Trusted Platform.
  4. Making It Easier for Our Users to Find Jobs.
